About Us!

We started out as a group in college in 2019 in an internet application class and decided to creat an online store as our project.
We then decided to take it to the next step and open our project as a legitimate business.

We are an online store that sells electronics, accesories, and games for many different consoles
such as the Playstation, Xbox, Nintendo, and PC.
We also have a delivery service that can deliver to your doorstep as fast as possible!
